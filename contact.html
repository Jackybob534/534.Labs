Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple Discord bots can be completed in a few days, while websites may take longer. We'll provide a detailed timeline with your quote.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! All projects include 30 days of free support. We also offer maintenance packages for long-term support, updates, and feature additions.

What's included in the project cost?

Our quotes include development, testing, deployment, documentation, and initial support. Hosting and third-party services are typically separate unless specified.

Can you work with existing systems?

More than likely yes! We can integrate with existing websites, databases, and Discord servers. We'll assess compatibility during consultation.

What if I need changes after completion?

Minor changes are included in our support period of up to 30 days. Larger modifications will be handled as separate projects.
