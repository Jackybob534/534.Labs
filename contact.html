Frequently Asked Questions

How long does a typical project take?

Project timelines vary based on complexity. Simple Discord bots can be completed in 3-5 days, while comprehensive ERLC websites may take 2-3 weeks. We'll provide a detailed timeline with your quote.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes! All projects include 30 days of free support. We also offer maintenance packages for long-term support, updates, and feature additions.

What's included in the project cost?

Our quotes include development, testing, deployment, documentation, and initial support. Hosting and third-party services are typically separate unless specified.

Can you work with existing systems?

Absolutely! We can integrate with existing websites, databases, Discord servers, and game systems. We'll assess compatibility during consultation.

Do you offer custom payment plans?

Yes, we offer flexible payment options including milestone-based payments for larger projects. Contact us to discuss payment arrangements that work for you.

What if I need changes after completion?

Minor changes are included in our support period. Larger modifications can be handled through our maintenance packages or as separate projects.
